Types of Plants and Their Life Cycle

    • Roses

  2. Non-Flowering Plants

  3. Succulents

    • Cacti
    • Aloe Vera
    • Jade Plant

Life Cycle of a Plant

  1. Seed
    • The plant life cycle begins with a seed.
  2. Germination
    • The seed absorbs water and begins to sprout.
  3. Growth
    • The plant grows leaves and stems.
  4. Reproduction
    • The plant produces flowers or cones to reproduce.
  5. Pollination
    • Pollen is transferred from one flower to another.
  6. Seed Dispersal
    • New seeds are spread to start the cycle again.
Photosynthesis
The process by which plants convert sunlight into energy.
Chlorophyll
The green pigment in plants that captures light energy.
Pollinators
Animals like bees and butterflies that help in the pollination process.
